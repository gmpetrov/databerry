### **What is GDPR?**

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union (EU) privacy law that allows EU citizens and residents to have access and control over their personal data.

- **Does it affect you?**
  If your business is based in the European Union (EU), or you process the personal data of individuals in the EU, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) affects you.

### **Is Chaindesk GDPR Compliant?**

Chaindesk is based in France (EU) and complies with the GDPR framework.

- **The measures we took**
  - Our [privacy policy](/privacy/cookie-policy) gives you more information about what data we collect, data retention and transfers, and outlines your data protection rights.
  - All Chaindesk chatbot data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and stored in France (EU)
  - Data accessibility: you have full control of the information you collect, store and manage with Chaindesk.
  - We have a Data Processing Agreement available for you. Email us at support@chaindesk.ai and will send it over.

### **What happens with form data?**

Chaindesk is the provider of a form service, and not the owner of the collected form responses.

- **The chatbot creator is responsible for the data he/she collects**
  ... and is thus data controller of the respondent data. Chaindesk is the processor and stores information on behalf of the chatbot creators. \*\*\*\*As long as your account is active you (as the chatbot creator) have full control over the data you collect, and the time period for which you store the data.
  You are able to delete or export form responses from your account if it would be required to do so. We honor all deletions, and all form data which has been deleted by you is permanently deleted from our back-ups within 90 days.

### **How do you use my personal data?**

Chaindesk acts as a data controller in the relationship between Chaindesk and our customers (chatbot creators), for the personal information you give us in order to use our service (registration information for example). Chaindesk does not sell personal data to third parties or use it for marketing purposes or for serving advertisements.

We only share your information with our service providers who help us operate our business, in which case those third parties are required to comply with the GDPR framework.
