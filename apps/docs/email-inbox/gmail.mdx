To configure an email forwarding setup with Gmail, follow these steps:

1. **Initiate Email Forwarding Configuration**
   - First, navigate to the email settings by selecting "See all settings" in the top right corner of your email interface.
     ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.45.40.png](/images/email-inbox/gmail/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.45.40.png)
2. **Add a Forwarding Address**
   - Go to the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab, then select "Add a forwarding address."
     ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.48.10.png](/images/email-inbox/gmail/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.48.10.png)
   - Input the email address you obtained from the Chaindesk Email Inbox settings. Then, proceed with "Next" and confirm by clicking "Proceed."
     ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.49.58.png](/images/email-inbox/gmail/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.49.58.png)
3. **Verification Process**
   - Sometimes, a verification email is sent by Gmail to confirm the forwarding address.
   - In your Chaindesk inbox, locate and open the confirmation email from Gmail. Follow the verification link within this email to complete the process.
4. **Create an Email Filter**
   - To ensure all emails are correctly forwarded to Chaindesk, you'll need to set up a filter.
   - Again, click "See all settings" in the top right, then navigate to "Filters and Blocked Addresses" and select "Create a new filter."
   - In the filter settings, enter your official support email in the "To" field. If you've verified a sender email address with Chaindesk, ensure it matches the address you're inputting here.
   - Proceed to "Create filter."
     ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-13.34.40.png](/images/email-inbox/gmail/Xnapper-2024-01-10-13.34.40.png)
5. **Configure Filter Settings**
   - In the filter options, check the box next to "Forward it to" and choose the forwarding address you previously set up.
   - Finalize this step by clicking "Create filter."
     ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-13.36.37.png](/images/email-inbox/gmail/Xnapper-2024-01-10-13.36.37.png)
