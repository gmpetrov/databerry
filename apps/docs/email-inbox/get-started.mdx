You can receive and send emails directly from Chaindesk.

Emails are working as redirections, forwarding for instance **contact@company.com** to your Chaindesk inbox. You will love it!

Follow the steps below to get started.

1.  **Create an email inbox**
    Create a new email inbox by selecting Email Inboxes from the navigation panel on the left and then click “New Email Inbox”.
    ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.28.30.png](/images/email-inbox/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.28.30.png)
2.  **Setup inbox**
    Choose an alias for your inbox.
    Then, optionally enter a custom sender email

        <Note>

    💡 Setting up a custom email is a paid feature. On the free plan, emails will be sent from **your-alias@on.chaindesk.ai**

            </Note>

            ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.29.28.png](/images/email-inbox/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.29.28.png)

            Once you have entered your sender address, click **Verify**.

            You will receive an email with a verification code from Chaindesk shortly.

3.  **Set up email forwarding**
    Finally, you need to forwarding incoming emails to your Chaindesk inbox.
    Click the **Install** tab and copy the forwarding address.

        ![Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.30.29.png](/images/email-inbox/Xnapper-2024-01-10-12.30.29.png)

        Then go to your email provider and set up forwarding to the address you copied.

        You can see how to do this for some popular email providers below.

        [Gmail](/email-inbox/gmail)
